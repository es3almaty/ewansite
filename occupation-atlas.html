AI-OS Occupation Atlas

Search an occupation. See the tasks inside it.

AI does not transform jobs evenly. It compresses some tasks, assists others, raises proof burdens, and leaves some forms of judgment, trust, and accountability more important than before.

This public tool provides a first-pass AI-OS interpretation of occupations as bundles of tasks. It is designed to move beyond crude “jobs at risk” claims and toward work redesign.

Core idea

Jobs are bundles. Tasks are the unit.

The public atlas shows representative tasks, task bundles, high-level AI-OS ratings, and likely future role directions.

The full AI-OS method goes deeper into governance, proof, residual effort, and role rebundling.
