AI-OS

A management system for AI, so your organization can adopt it without losing control of risk, accountability, or direction.
The problem

AI is being used in your organization. Different teams use different tools. No one is sure what is working, what is not, or where the risks sit. One team writes a policy. Another buys a license. A third quietly experiments.

The result is wasted spend, hidden risk, and missed gains. The problem is not the tools. The problem is that there is no system for managing them.

What we do

We work with your leadership to design and stand up a simple operating system for AI. It defines who decides what. It sets out where AI is allowed, where it is not, and under what conditions. It builds in risk management proportionate to what is at stake. It makes progress visible, so you can tell what is actually working.

AI-OS builds on the AI Matrix as its foundation, so the operating system is grounded in a clear view of where you currently are.

What you get
  • An operating model for how AI fits into your organization
  • Decision rules for who uses AI, where, and under what conditions
  • Risk and accountability frameworks that fit your existing governance
  • A practical roadmap with milestones you can hold people to
Who this is for

Companies and corporate groups beginning serious AI adoption. Public sector bodies that need governance before scale. Universities running their own AI integration. Any organization that knows it needs to move on AI, and does not want to do it badly.

Already built

The AI-OS framework is published as an open-access working paper, setting out the four-component architecture: the AI Matrix, the 3A Model, the Decision Compass, and the OI orchestration layer.
