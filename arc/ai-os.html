The problem

What this fixes

AI is already being used in your organization. Different teams use different tools. No one is sure what is working, what is risky, or what should be scaled.

One team writes a policy. Another buys a license. A third quietly experiments. The result is wasted spend, hidden risk, and missed gains.

What we do

How the engagement works

We help leadership design and stand up an operating system for AI.

AI-OS defines who decides what, where AI is permitted, what controls apply, how residual human effort is estimated, and how roles should be rebundled.

The work starts at the task layer, because task-level visibility is where adoption, control, and productivity actually meet.

What you get

Outputs

  • A task register and AI-mode model for priority functions
  • Decision rules for who uses AI, where, and under what conditions
  • Risk, proof, and accountability controls that fit existing governance
  • A role-rebundling roadmap with capacity implications

Who this is for

Client fit

Companies, universities, public sector bodies, and leadership teams that need AI adoption to become manageable, auditable, and useful.
