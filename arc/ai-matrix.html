The problem

What this fixes

AI adoption headlines hide the real picture. High adoption rates can mask passive use, where people have access to tools but no real skill or authority to use them well.

Low adoption rates can mask sophisticated use by a small group. Without a clear view of where you actually are, investment goes in the wrong places.

What we do

How the engagement works

We map your position on two dimensions: access and agency.

Access measures tools, data, infrastructure, and reach. Agency measures skills, decision rights, confidence, and the ability to use AI well.

The result is a practical baseline for action, not a decorative heatmap.

What you get

Outputs

  • A diagnostic of your current position across access and agency
  • A clear view of where the two dimensions are out of balance
  • A risk and investment priority map
  • A baseline you can track year on year

Who this is for

Client fit

Government ministries, universities, companies with multi-unit operations, development organizations, and leadership teams that need a realistic view of AI capability.
