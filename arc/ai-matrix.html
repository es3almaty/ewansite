AI Matrix

A diagnostic that shows where your organization actually stands on AI, and where the real gaps are.
The problem

AI adoption headlines hide the real picture. High adoption rates can mask passive use, where people have access to tools but no real skill or authority to use them well. Low adoption rates can mask sophisticated use by a small group.

Without a clear view of where you actually are, investment goes in the wrong places, and the people most at risk of being left behind are the hardest to see.

What we do

We map your position on two dimensions. Access measures who has the tools, the data, and the infrastructure to use AI. Agency measures who has the skills, the decision rights, and the confidence to use it well.

The two dimensions produce four quadrants, from full empowerment, where access and agency are both high, to full dependency, where neither is in place. The diagnostic works at the level of an individual, a team, an organization, or a country.

What you get
  • A diagnostic of your current position across access and agency
  • A clear view of where the two dimensions are out of balance
  • A risk and investment priority map
  • A baseline you can track year on year
Who this is for

Government ministries running national AI strategies. Universities and research institutions. Companies and corporate groups with operations across multiple business units or countries. Development organizations funding AI capacity work.

Already built

AI Matrix Live is the open dashboard built on this framework. It tracks 115 countries on access and agency using Anthropic Economic Index data, updated with every release.
