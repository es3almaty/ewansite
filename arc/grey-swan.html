Service logic

Scenario discipline

Grey Swan work is not a crystal ball. It is a disciplined way to move strategic discussion from vibes to explicit probabilities, evidence, and update rules.

Live framework ARC overview AI-OS examples

The problem

What this fixes

Most foresight is either loose expert opinion or expensive private modeling. Both can help, but neither always produces a defensible trail of assumptions.

Leaders making long-term calls need probabilities they can challenge, update, and defend.

What we do

How the engagement works

The public Grey Swan framework runs as a structured probability exercise. ARC engagements layer sector or country context on top.

We produce sector-specific cuts, country-specific cuts, and retainer arrangements for organizations that need structured updates between full runs.

The value lies in explicit assumptions, movement limits, and evidence-linked interpretation.

What you get

Outputs

  • A custom sector or country cut of the Grey Swan framework
  • A defensible evidence base for strategic decisions
  • Briefings tailored to leadership questions
  • Optional retainer access for quarterly updates

Who this is for

Client fit

Government strategy units, sovereign wealth funds, development organizations, think tanks, and corporate strategy teams making decisions on a 5 to 25 year horizon.
