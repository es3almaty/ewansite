ARC  ·  Foresight

Grey Swan

Sector-specific and country-specific foresight engagements using the Grey Swan framework, on commission.
The problem

Most foresight is either expert opinion or expensive private modeling. Both have their place. Neither produces evidence you can audit, reproduce, or use to defend a decision.

Leaders making long-term calls on energy, education, health, AI, or trade need probabilities they can trust, and a framework that updates when the world actually changes.

What we do

The public Grey Swan framework runs on a six-month cadence and produces a global Spring and Autumn briefing for free. ARC engagements layer on top of that public work. We produce sector-specific cuts (such as energy, finance, education, defense) and country-specific cuts that map global indicators to a local decision context.

For organizations that need ongoing access between full runs, we offer retainer arrangements that include quarterly check-ins, custom indicator tracking, and direct briefings on findings as they emerge.

What you get
  • A custom sector or country cut of the Grey Swan framework
  • A defensible evidence base for strategic decisions
  • Briefings tailored to your leadership team's questions
  • Optional retainer access for ongoing strategic work between full runs
Who this is for

Government strategy units. Sovereign wealth funds and long-horizon investors. Development organizations and think tanks. Corporate strategy teams making decisions on a 5 to 25 year horizon. Anyone who needs foresight that holds up under serious scrutiny.

Already built

The Grey Swan framework runs publicly on a six-monthly cadence. Currently at v11.9 with the Spring 2026 run published in full. All commissioned work uses this framework as its foundation.
