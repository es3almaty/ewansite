OI

A practical method for getting real productivity and quality gains out of AI, by training people to design structured workflows with it.
The problem

Your people are using AI. The outputs are inconsistent. The time savings are unclear. The work AI is supposed to make better often comes back worse, or just different.

The reason is almost always the same. AI is being used as a search tool or a writing aid, when the real gains come from designed workflows where the human acts as the orchestrator and not the typist.

What we do

We train your teams to design and run structured human-AI workflows for the work that matters most. That means clear framing, multi-stage prompting, quality checks built into the process, and clear handoffs between human judgment and AI output.

The method is grounded in the IHACC framework, which treats knowledge work as a designed cycle between human intent, AI processing, and iterative review.

What you get
  • A capability program your teams can apply within weeks
  • Workflow templates for the work that matters most
  • A common language and method across teams
  • Real, measurable improvements in speed and quality of output
Who this is for

Knowledge-intensive teams. Strategy, research, communications, legal, policy, finance. Professional services firms. Government policy units. University research offices. Any team where the output is thinking, written, or analyzed work.

Already built
