The problem

What this fixes

Your people are using AI. The outputs are inconsistent. The time savings are unclear. Work often comes back faster, but not necessarily better.

The usual reason is simple: AI is treated as a search tool or writing aid, when the real gains come from designed workflows.

What we do

How the engagement works

We train teams to design and run structured human-AI workflows for the work that matters most.

That means better framing, staged prompting, quality checks, source discipline, and clear handoffs between AI output and human judgment.

The method is grounded in Orchestrated Intelligence and the IHACC model.

What you get

Outputs

  • A capability program your teams can apply within weeks
  • Workflow templates for priority knowledge work
  • A common language for human-AI collaboration
  • Measurable improvements in speed, quality, and reuse

Who this is for

Client fit

Knowledge-intensive teams: strategy, research, communications, legal, policy, finance, professional services, government policy units, and university research offices.
