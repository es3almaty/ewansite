The natural arc of an AI engagement

Mapping comes first: a clear view of access, agency, current use, risk, and gaps. Implementation puts a management system around AI. Augmentation trains people to design better work with AI, rather than simply prompting harder. Education protects proof where learning and credentials are at stake. Foresight connects today’s decisions to medium-term strategic uncertainty.
